**Apr 18, 2023.** MLRG member Xi Zhang presented the group’s work titled "*Flood Inundation Meets Image Super-Resolution*" at the conference *AI in Agriculture: Innovation and Discovery to Equitably Meet Producer Needs and Perceptions* (AI in Ag 2023), held in Orlando, Florida.

<!--more-->

The presentation highlighted our work on combining flood inundation analysis with image super-resolution methods, with the goal of improving the spatial detail and usefulness of remotely sensed flood information. This line of research reflects MLRG’s broader interest in machine learning methods for environmental and geospatial applications.

Congratulations to Xi on representing MLRG at AI in Ag 2023!
