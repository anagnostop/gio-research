**Dec 21, 2025.** We are pleased to welcome Prerak Patel to our research group.

<!--more-->

Prerak is pursuing an M.S. in Computer Engineering at Florida Tech. He brings interests in artificial intelligence, machine learning, time-series analysis, high-performance computing, and edge systems. These interests align well with several of our group’s current research directions, especially our work on machine learning for environmental and earth-science applications.

In the group, Prerak will contribute to our ongoing NASA/SERVIR project on generative precipitation nowcasting. This project focuses on developing machine-learning methods for short-term precipitation forecasting, with particular emphasis on generative and uncertainty-aware approaches that can support flood warning and hydrometeorological decision-making.

Over time, Prerak is expected to take on a significant role in advancing this effort, including model development, experimentation, evaluation, and the broader research workflow needed to move the project forward.

Welcome to the group, Prerak!
